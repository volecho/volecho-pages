Entre Tes Mains: Antonio David Bridges Emotion and Innovation

Volecho.com Published on January 17, 2026

The following feature is now included in our online magazine which is also available in print.

Issue #10

Online Magazine | Print Magazine

Antonio David, a French singer-songwriter, comes back with a very intimate track, “Entre tes mains,” released on January 5th, 2026. Born in Angers, David has produced a track that exudes a great deal of soul and introspection, as well as a desire to push the boundaries offered by modern production techniques.

Completely recorded in his hometown using only his phone, “Entre tes mains” is a lesson in subtlety and restraint. Underlying it all, of course, is a meeting of soothing piano and passionate saxophone that is as immediate as it is eternal. Every note is precisely placed to complement the lyrics that map the contours of longing and regret without ever venturing into soap operatic territory.



What is unique about this release is Antonio's use of technology for artistic purposes. Working with AI technology that assists with lyrics, Antonio combines his experiences with modern technology, creating a story that is specific, poignant, and meaningful. The combination of human emotion with technology assistance comes off as intentional rather than novelty-seeking, which is a testament to an artist open to innovation yet grounded in storytelling.

*Entre tes mains* is a prime example of a balance between vulnerability and complexity. The vocal performance of David is both delicate and refined, moving the story forward with a sense of truth. The interplay of the sax and piano creates a sense of grandeur to frame the song, drawing the listener into a space where the emotion can be experienced to the fullest. The arrangement of the song has a cinematic quality to it, much like a jazz ballad, yet it is very much rooted in the time it is created.

For those listeners who enjoy music which is both reflective and well-crafted, *Entre Tes Mains* will prove to be a compelling listen. The minimalistic recording style, combined with the innovative writing of lyrics, indicates a confident artist who isn’t afraid to take a different route. Antonio David remains a testament to the fact that songwriting from the heart and experimenting with technology do not have to be at odds. "Entre tes mains" is a highlight in his journey so far—a single that lingers long after its last note has faded away.

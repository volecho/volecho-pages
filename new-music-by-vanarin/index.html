VANARIN’s Mesmerizing New Release: A Taste of Their Upcoming Third Album

January 7, 2025 by Volecho.com

Follow Our Playlist For More Music!

As anticipation builds for VANARIN’s highly awaited third album under the Dischi Sotterranei label, the British-Italian band continues to make waves across the music world. Already earning accolades and securing their spot among the most compelling acts of the moment, VANARIN’s recent feature on the prestigious BBC platforms has solidified their rising-star status. Now, to ease the wait for their next big release, the band is gifting fans with a new single “What We Said”.


This latest track is a mesmerizing psych ballad, anchored by an entrancing piano progression that takes center stage. Layered atop is the frontman’s hypnotic voice, which weaves a spellbinding rhythm, undulating and intoxicating as it explores the haunting aftermath of a love lost. Like a mantra, the lyrics obsessively question the meaning behind a lover’s parting words, plunging the listener into a dreamy yet poignant introspection.



Fans of Tame Impala’s Currents will feel the familiar pull of melancholic yet circular lyrics, while those who appreciate The Beatles’ experimental era will be drawn to the artful manipulation and pitch-shifted vocal sections. But that’s not all—the track’s lush sonic landscape is further enriched by a warm, soulful bass line and vintage-inspired guitar phrases that seem to echo straight out of the 1960s.

What truly sets VANARIN apart is their meticulous attention to detail. Every element of “What We Said” unfolds with a fresh, unexpected twist, creating a soundscape that feels innovative yet timeless. It’s this masterful balance that makes their signature style unmistakable and keeps their audience coming back for more.

With such a tantalizing preview, VANARIN’s third album promises to be a standout moment in contemporary music—one that fans and critics alike will be eager to experience.

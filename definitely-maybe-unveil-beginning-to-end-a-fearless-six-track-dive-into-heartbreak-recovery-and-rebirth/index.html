Definitely Maybe Unveil Beginning to End, a Fearless Six-Track Dive Into Heartbreak, Recovery, and Rebirth

Volecho.com Published on November 24, 2025

Chicago’s rising alt-rock duo Definitely Maybe return with Beginning to End, a striking six-song EP that traces the emotional arc of losing love and slowly learning to live again. Produced by hitmaker Kevin McCombs (Sueco, Papa Roach, Story of the Year), the project blends alt-rock grit, emo-pop vulnerability, and a sharp pop-punk bite — a fusion that turns each track into its own cinematic chapter of heartbreak and healing.

On Beginning to End, the band leans fully into honesty. Each song captures a different facet of emotional fallout: the initial sting, the spiraling questions, the moments of denial, and finally, the fragile but powerful return to hope. Towering choruses, explosive guitars, and raw, expressive vocals form the backbone of the EP, weaving a narrative that feels both deeply personal and universally familiar.

“Beginning to End is about processing everything — the pain, the denial, the acceptance — and finding some kind of hope at the other side,” the duo explains. “It’s the most honest thing we’ve made yet.”

With its blend of visceral storytelling and modern, arena-ready production, Beginning to End positions Definitely Maybe as one of 2026’s essential new rock-pop acts. The EP lands November 14, 2025 via DMG Recordings, marking a defining moment for a band stepping boldly into their sound — and into their future.

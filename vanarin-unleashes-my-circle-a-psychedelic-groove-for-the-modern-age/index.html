Vanarin Unleashes "My Circle": A Psychedelic Groove for the Modern Age


November 27th marks the arrival of Vanarin’s electrifying new single, My Circle, the sixth teaser from their much-anticipated album. As the Anglo-Italian quartet, signed to Dischi Sotterranei, gears up for the album's release, this track promises to thrill fans of both urban beats and cutting-edge psychedelia alike.

Follow Vanarin

Fully immersed in a modern pop sound, My Circle is a sonic adventure that marries an explosive electronic beat with a sixties-inspired bassline. Enveloping drums create an irresistible, sinuous rhythm that feels like pure motion, while the vocal line deftly navigates the space between contemporary R&B and jazzy harmonics. It’s a bold invitation to move, groove, and get lost in the beat—a track impossible to resist.

Lyrically, the song leans into minimalist storytelling, perfectly aligning with its hypnotic soundscape. The band explains, “My Circle describes the struggle to embrace technological and behavioral changes in society, polluting the thinking and interactions once had with friends and family.” The theme resonates deeply, adding weight to the infectious melody.


Vanarin—composed of David Paysden, Marco Sciacqua, Massimo Mantovani, and Marco Brena—may hail from Anglo-Italian roots, but their compositional brilliance places them firmly alongside Australia’s psychedelic heavyweights like Tame Impala, Pond, and Mildlife. This latest single solidifies their ability to fuse refined soundscapes with bold, contemporary ideas.

Mix and Mastering: Sonic Alchemy
The production on My Circle is a masterclass in balance and texture. The electronic beat drives the track with pulsating energy, while the retro bassline and nuanced drumming create a dynamic interplay that feels both nostalgic and futuristic. Vocals glide effortlessly over the mix, with just the right amount of reverb and clarity to keep them at the heart of the groove. This fusion of retro and modern elements makes the track a standout in today’s music landscape.

Movie Pairing for the Mood
Pair My Circle with the visual journey of Her (2013). The film’s exploration of technology’s impact on human relationships mirrors the song’s themes, while its dreamy, futuristic aesthetic complements the track’s lush, psychedelic sound. Together, they create an experience that is both thought-provoking and immersive.
